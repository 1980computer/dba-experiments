Day 60: Open and Close Snaps

SVG Module and Clip

The circular clipping effect seen in Snapchat is quite interesting. I've refered to Hyengjoo Nam's delightful example of a SVG spinner to create this demo today. The base SVG module was created by Josh Puckett works great for creating the clipping spinner seen in Snapchat. Click "My Story" to view my snap and the time spinner, then drag on the snap to view the clipping effect.

I was inspired by Peter Gr's recreation of the New York Time's interactive widget on Usain Bolt's Olympic start time. Upon examining Peter's work, I found his use of performance.now() quite interesting and decided to create a similar timer with real-time time updates.

Day 54: Facebook Video

.placeBehind, scroll, .onMove, and Faking Content inside Components

Today I wanted to do something I've seen for quite a bit: Facebook's iPad app video pop-up interaction. I've repurposed it for FB's iPhone app by making the in-feed video the same as regular link thumbnails - i.e. without being full-bleed. I've kept the auto-play when scrolling feature. Tap the video to see it fullscreen, tap again or drag it to close it.

Day 53: SoundCloud Player

Convert Second to MM:SS, Variables, Loops, Utils.Interval and String Interpolation

I've very much enjoyed SoundCloud's iOS experience since it was updated last year. For today's project, I decided to recreate it. It turned out to be much more complex than I had anticipated. The prototype is a bit slow and laggy due to the three Utils.interval functions I used to keep track of time and change the music bar background color.

Nested Loops, String to Integer, Animations, States, and Drag
