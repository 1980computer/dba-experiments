Day 8

More Videos and Volume

For today's prototype I wanted to keep it simple. I wanted to emulate the video volume effect on New York Times' iPad app. When the page is scroll while a video is playing, the video's volume gradually fades as the page is scrolled further away. When the volume becomes 0, the video pauses playing. It resumes when the user scrolls back up.

View Prototype

I found Jackie Chui's prototype on a different scrubber to be immensely useful in figuring out how to manipulate the video events with JS.
