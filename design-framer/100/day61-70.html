Day 68: Google Photos Transitions

.onPinch and randomize Utility Function

I wanted to recreate the smooth photo grid zoom interaction seen in the Google Photos app. This one turned out to be a hard nut to crack. The origin interaction had a smooth scaling of not only the photos but the margin space as well - the scaled amount of the two are indepedent but the overall photo grid scales in proportion. Also the photos near the center of the pinch retain their location, so only the photos around them are scaled and translated. After looking into this for an entire day, this is as close as I can get. Mobile device preview only. Pinch to view the effect.

Day 66: Page Swipe

Dummy Animation as Event Trigger, and .draggable

This prototype was again inspired by the Hyper app. Built-in page component doesn't work with the fixed dragging, that is while the layer is not moving on screen, a drag event detects finger swipe, causing the said layer to change (eg. in scale, color, etc., an attribute other than position). Instead I built invisible dragging areas and mapped their changes to the page layers. I also used a dummy animation to keep track of the title bar fade-ins and fade-outs, instead of using Utils.delay. Swipe to view interaction.
